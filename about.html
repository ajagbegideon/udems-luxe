Who We Are

A Trusted &
Reliable Brand

Udems Luxe Collections is a trusted and reliable family-owned luggage business based in Balogun, Lagos Island. We proudly serve customers across Nigeria with premium quality luggage for school use, fashion, office use and commercial purposes.

Over the years we have built a strong goodwill for quality products, excellent customer service and reliable products trusted by thousands of customers across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reach out to more than 40% of Nigeria's population ensuring they are served good quality bags, boxes and more to elevate their confidence and save costs.
