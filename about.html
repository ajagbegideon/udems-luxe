Who We Are

A Trusted &
Reliable Brand

Udems Luxe Collections is an extension of M.I.C Godswill Nig. Ltd., which has been in existence since the 90s and is a trusted and reliable family-owned business based in Broad Street, Lagos Island. We proudly serve customers across Nigeria with premium-quality luggage for school use, fashion, office use and commercial purposes.

Over the years we have built strong goodwill for quality products, pocket-friendly prices, excellent customer service and reliable products trusted by thousands of customers across Nigeria.

Our Mission

Serving Nigeria
With Quality

Our mission is to reach out to more than 40% of Nigeria's population ensuring they are served good quality bags, boxes and more to elevate their confidence and save costs.

We are committed to making premium quality bags and luggage accessible and affordable for everyone across Nigeria.
